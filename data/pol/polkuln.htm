Mounted Uhlan

Low melee damage, hand-to-hand combat only, quickly gains experience.

The word for this type of light cavalry, "uhlan", comes from the Tatar "oglan" which means "the bold". It is an extremely crucial unit because of its speed and low training costs.

Although these units can fight only in close combat, they quickly gain experience for killed enemy soldiers and increase their personal combat indexes with that.

Good for outflanking the enemy troops and wiping out peasants.
