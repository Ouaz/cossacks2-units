Mounted Cuirassier

High melee damage, pistols (double-shot), effectively demoralizes enemy in melee, is hard to shoot.

Heavy mounted soldier wearing cuirasses, which are made of two leather or metal plates bent around the chest or back and then braced together.

They were very courageous warriors and eroded the morale of the enemy soldiers with a single glance on their shiny and rich armors. Thus, the outcome of the battle may often depend on these soldiers.

This and their high hand-to-hand combat indexes and 2 pistols make them an important part of the battlefields.
