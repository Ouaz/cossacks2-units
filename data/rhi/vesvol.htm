Voltiger

Weak in hand-to-hand combat, good shot, light coal consumption when shooting, highly resistant to fatigue, fast training speed.

Voltigers (from "to flit" in French) were considered to be elite troops that were formed of the soldiers who distinguished themselves as good shooters.

They have a higher endurance than the other soldiers due to less bulky dragoon muskets in their equipment, they also use bayonets and half-sabers.

They are great shooters, capable of substantially decreasing the enemy's troop morale on great distance.
