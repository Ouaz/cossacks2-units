Light Cavalryman

Low melee damage, quickly gains experience.

Light cavalry has always been in great demand in Napoleon's army and especially in the armies of his allies. The Westfalen light cavalry regiment can be considered the best light cavalry of the German allies of France.

It was part of the 1st Victor's corps and later on, it was transferred to the 4th corps of Sebastian. Its first commander was Colonel von Hammerstein.

The regiment won fame in important clashes and battles.
