Mounted Hussar

Average melee damage, short rifle (case-shot).

Hussar (Hungarian - "huszár" means "light horseman") is a type of soldier in any European light cavalry unit in the 18th and 19th centuries who adopted an ornate uniform similar to the one of the Hungarian cavalry of the 15th century.

Hussars are fast moving, they have good melee damage and use case-shot loaded musketoons, that injure more units with a single shot.
