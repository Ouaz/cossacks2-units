Mounted Guard

Very high melee damage, uses rifle, effectively demoralizes enemy in melee, increased level of health.

The Spanish army had way too many dragoon regiments, they were the basic shock troops of the Spanish cavalry. Like dragoon regiments of the other countries, these were used virtually as the cavalry, notwithstanding the name.

The Mounted Guard, also known as the Prince cavalry regiment, belonged to General de La Romana. In 1807, it was sent to Denmark, where it attracted lots of attention by its eccentric behavior.

They were the first in Denmark to smoke cigars. According to the witnesses, everyone starting from young boy drummers to generals continuously smoked cigars.
