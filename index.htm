  • Life : Total life points for the unit.
  • Melee : Damage infliged by one melee hit. Does not include formation, standard bearer, or XP bonuses.
  • Ranged : Damage infliged by one fire shot (reduced with distance).
  • Morale : Starting morale value with all morale bonuses:
    • a full infantry squad (+25) with officer, drummer and standard bearer (+30)
    • a full cavalry squad (+9) with mounted standard bearer (+10),
    • a full specialist squad (+3).
    • A squad will start to flee with a moral value of 30.
  • Training : Time required to create one unit ; in gameframes [divide by the game refresh rate (50 or 60) for time in seconds].
  • Coal : Coal consumption for one shot.
  • Reload : Time required to reload after one weapon shot ; in gameframes [divide by the game refresh rate (50 or 60) for time in seconds].
